Best Hotels in Narok Town 2026, Complete Guide to Accommodation in Narok County

By Narok Travel Guide | Updated April 2026 | 7 min read

Narok Town is the gateway to Maasai Mara and one of Kenya's most visited transit points for safari travellers. But Narok County is far more than a stopover, it is a destination in its own right, home to some of Kenya's most breathtaking landscapes, Maasai culture, and increasingly, world-class hospitality.

If you are searching for the best hotel in Narok, this guide covers your options honestly, and tells you exactly why one resort stands above all others in the region.

#1 Pick, Best Hotel in Narok County

★★★★★

#1, Sidai Resort & Hotel, Naroosura

For travellers willing to go 35km beyond Narok Town into the Naroosura area, Sidai Resort & Hotel offers an experience that no hotel in Narok Town itself can match. Set on the slopes of the Loita Hills with a swimming pool, 11 accommodation rooms, two event halls, a full restaurant, bonfires and birdwatching, Sidai Resort is the best hotel in Narok County by a significant margin.

The price is genuinely affordable. The location is extraordinary. The service, as the name Sidai (Excellence in Maasai) suggests, is exceptional.

What to Look For in a Narok Hotel

Before booking accommodation in Narok County, assess five things clearly: location, price, amenities, food quality and access to nature. If you are traveling for business, add event facilities to your checklist.

By those standards, Sidai Resort consistently leads. You get a scenic destination setting, strong room value, a complete dining menu, pool access, and professionally managed event spaces in one place.
