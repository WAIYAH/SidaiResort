Where Exactly is Naroosura?

Naroosura is a small but beautiful town in Narok County, Kenya, located in the shadow of the Loita Hills, approximately 35km from Narok Town. It sits within the broader Maasai Mara ecosystem, making it one of the most scenically stunning locations in the entire country.

The Loita Hills that surround Naroosura are cool, forested and teeming with wildlife and birdlife, a world apart from the heat and dust of the savanna. Naroosura is still a hidden gem on Kenya's tourism map, which makes Sidai Resort all the more special: a full-service hotel in one of Kenya's most beautiful and unspoiled locations.

Why Sidai Resort is the Best Hotel in Naroosura

1. The Setting is Breathtaking

Sidai Resort sits directly on the slopes of the Loita Hills, a cool, forested environment where temperatures are gentler than the surrounding lowlands. The grounds are lush, birdsong is constant, and the view from the balcony at sunset is the kind that makes you immediately extend your stay.

2. The Accommodation, 11 Named Sanctuaries

Sidai Resort offers 11 uniquely named rooms, each bearing a Maasai name, each designed for genuine comfort. From Emirishoi to the premium Elalai suite, every room is well-appointed, clean, and designed with the Loita Hills landscape in mind. Waking up to the sound of birds in the Naroosura forest is something you simply cannot put a price on.

3. The Swimming Pool

One of the few resorts in Narok County with a full swimming pool, Sidai Resort's pool is pristine, spacious, and surrounded by the natural forest environment. Whether you come as a day guest or a resident, the pool is a destination in itself.

4. Food & Nyama Choma

Sidai Resort's kitchen draws from Kenya's rich culinary traditions with a menu that runs from full English breakfasts to authentic Maasai-style nyama choma. The goat roast is, by wide consensus, the finest nyama choma experience in Naroosura. Order it. You will not regret it.

5. Events, Conferences & Celebrations

Sidai Resort houses two professional event halls, Enchula and Entumo, making it the leading conference and events venue in Narok County. Weddings, birthday parties, corporate retreats, sherehe, all handled with the full force of Sidai's nothing but the best philosophy.

6. The Bonfire & Sundowner Experience

As the Loita Hills sky turns gold and the stars begin to appear, Sidai Resort's signature bonfire experience, Enkima, gathers guests around an open fire for an evening of stories, warmth and genuine African magic. The sundowners from the resort balcony are equally extraordinary.

7. The Price, Exceptional Value

For a resort of this quality in such a stunning location, Sidai Resort's pricing is genuinely exceptional. Rooms from KSh 1,000, meals from KSh 100, and experiences that would cost ten times more elsewhere in Kenya's tourism circuit.

How to Get to Sidai Resort Naroosura

From Nairobi: Take the Nairobi-Narok highway (approx 180km, 3 hours). From Narok Town, follow the Naroosura road for approximately 35km. Sidai Resort is signposted on arrival.

From JKIA: Drive through Nairobi, join the Southern Bypass, proceed to Narok via Mai Mahiu or Rongai, then onwards to Naroosura.

GPS / Google Maps: Search Sidai Resort Naroosura Narok Kenya or call 0703 761 951 for directions on arrival.
