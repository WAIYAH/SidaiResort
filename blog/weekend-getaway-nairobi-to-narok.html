Weekend Getaway from Nairobi to Narok 2026

By Narok Travel Guide | Published April 30, 2026

Need a short break from Nairobi without complicated logistics? Narok County is one of the best weekend options in Kenya: scenic routes, cooler highland pockets, strong food culture and quick access to both nature and relaxation.

Friday Departure Plan

Leave Nairobi in the afternoon, use the Narok highway corridor, and continue toward Naroosura for check-in at Sidai Resort & Hotel. Evening arrival gives you time for a relaxed dinner and overnight rest.

Saturday Plan

Start with breakfast, then choose between pool sessions, local nature walks, farm visits or a regional day circuit. Return before sunset for Loita Hills balcony views and nyama choma dinner. Sidai's pace makes it ideal for couples, groups and solo reset trips.

Sunday Plan

Enjoy a lighter morning, optional birdwatching, and lunch before driving back to Nairobi. This pattern keeps road fatigue low while still giving you a real destination experience.
